2. Gambling losses must be itemized on your tax return. They cannot be claimed as a standard deduction.

3. Gambling losses must be substantiated with documentation. This includes receipts for tickets or other evidence of the amount of your loss.

It's important to keep track of your gambling wins and losses throughout the year, so you can accurately report them on your tax return. If you do have gambling income and losses, it's always a good idea to speak with a tax professional to make sure you're claiming everything correctly.
